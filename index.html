Why It Matters

The Problem vs. Our Solution

The Problem
  • ✕

    Zero Real-Time Visibility

    Legacy loggers record data locally with no live alerts, leaving excursions undetected for hours — or days.

  • ✕

    Manual, Error-Prone Processes

    Staff must physically retrieve and download data from each device — slow, unreliable, and impossible to scale.

  • ✕

    Rigid, Proprietary Hardware

    Closed ecosystems lock operators into expensive vendor contracts with no customization or integration options.

  • ✕

    Massive Spoilage & Cost

    Missed temperature excursions result in entire shipments of perishable goods being written off — a direct hit to the bottom line.

Our Solution
  • ✓

    Continuous Live Monitoring

    Sensors stream data in real time to a cloud dashboard — excursions trigger instant alerts for immediate action.

  • ✓

    Fully Automated Pipeline

    Data flows automatically from sensor to cloud — no manual downloads, no human error, fully scalable.

  • ✓

    Open & Flexible Architecture

    Modular design with standard protocols (BLE, LPWAN) means easy integration and zero vendor lock-in.

  • ✓

    Waste Elimination & ROI

    Proactive alerts prevent spoilage before it happens, dramatically cutting product loss and operational expenses.

System Architecture

How It Works

A four-stage pipeline that transforms raw sensor readings into actionable intelligence — fully automated, end to end.

01

Sense

Temperature & humidity sensors continuously monitor environmental conditions inside refrigerated trucks in real time.

02

Process

An onboard microcontroller (MCU) collects readings, performs edge processing, and checks against critical threshold violations.

03

Transmit

Data is transmitted wirelessly via Bluetooth or LPWAN (low-power, long-range technology) to a centralized cloud platform.

04

Alert

The cloud platform analyzes incoming data on a live dashboard and automatically generates alerts for immediate intervention.
